About Us

Welcome to Wild Circus !!

Our story begins more than 20 years ago in the heart of the Cevennes country. Several artists come together to found the Compagnie Wild circus, craft factory of universes of all kinds ...

After a few years of street shows all over Europe, the company is expanding and developing an atypical style mixing traditional circus codes with contemporary writings imbued with humor and poetry. Technical virtuosity meets the unusual, the sweet, the acid. Ingenuity is deployed in the service of the diversion of objects and the development of new equipment.
